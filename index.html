How to Brew with a French Fress

Ingredients:

  • French Press
  • boiling water
  • mug
  • Your Favorite Coffee, ground
  • sweetener
  • cream/milk

Directions:

Place ground coffee in an empty French Press.

The size of your Press will dictate how much coffee you will use. For a 12oz Press, use at least 2 tbsp of coffee. If you are using a bigger Press, adjust accordingly.

Pour boiling water into Press; fill about 3/4 of the way full. Leave AT LEAST 1″ of space from the top of the glass.

Next, use a plastic spoon (and ONLY a plastic spoon) to stir grounds.

Let the “coffee” sit for at least 5 minutes.

The longer you let it sit, the stronger it’ll be.

While coffee is "brewing," prepare mug by adding your choice of sweetener and milk.

Add your lid/plunger onto the Press glass and gently press down on the plunger; this will push the grounds to the bottom while straining your coffee.

Slowly pour the coffee into mug, stir your coffee mixture, and ENJOY!!
